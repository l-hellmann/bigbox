These are CC0 placeholder art (see CLAUDE.md "Custom art is out of scope").
Each character-{a..r}.glb is a rigged, node-animated blocky humanoid sharing a
common animation set (idle / walk / sprint / holding-both-shoot / attack / die /
…); textures live in textures/texture-{a..r}.png (the glbs' embedded URIs were
patched from the pack's original `Textures/` to match this lowercase dir).
